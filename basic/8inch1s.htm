8インチ片面単密度ディスクフォーマッタ


物理フォーマットはMS-DOSで可能
8"1Sディスクの需要ある？		 

注意事項と覚書き
・フォーマットに失敗する場合は
　MS-DOSで物理フォーマットするとよい

100 CLS
110 PRINT "8 inch 1S FORMATTER"
120 PRINT " "
130 PRINT "MOUNT A NEW DISK (8 1S) ON DRIVE"
140 INPUT "DRIVE#";DFR
200 PRINT "FORMATTING"
210 FIELD #0,128 AS A$, 128 AS B$
220 LSET A$=STRING$(128,CHR$(&HFF))
230 LSET B$=STRING$(128,CHR$(&HFF))
240 FOR TRK=0 TO 76
250  FOR SECS=1 TO 13
260  DSKO$ DTR,TRK,2*SECS-1
270  NEXT SECS
280 NEXT TRK
300 PRINT "MAKING FAT"
310 FIELD #0, 35 AS A$,1 AS B$,220 AS C$
320 LSET A$=STRING$(35,CHR$(&HFF))
330 LSET B$=STRING$(1,CHR$(&HFE))
340 LSET C$=STRING$(220,CHR$(&HFF))
350 FOR SECS=11 TO 13
360  DSKO$ DTR,35,2*SECS-1
370 NEXT SECS
400 PRINT "MAKING ID"
410 FIELD #0, 1 AS A$,1 AS B$, 254 AS C$
420 LSET A$=STRING$(1,CHR$(&H00))
430 LSET B$=STRING$(1,CHR$(&HFF))
440 LSET C$=STRING$(254,CHR$(&H20))
450 DSKO$ DTR,35,19
500 END
